Why VASUDHA

Regeneration cannot be certified
by those who have never practised it.

LEED and GRIHA measure compliance against Western industrial baselines. VASUDHA measures regeneration against ecological truth — using knowledge systems that predate the buildings they assess. This is not a reaction. It is a return.

  • Points for low-flow fixtures Hydrological integration with local aquifer and recharge
  • Energy Performance Index compliance Agro-climatic passive design — no baseline required
  • Material credits for certified products Ethnoscientific materials — lime, husk, ash, earth
